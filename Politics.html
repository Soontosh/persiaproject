Politics

The leader of New Jersey is the governor. His name is Phil Murphy, a Democrat who was freely elected by the people. The type of government is a constitutional federal republic. This means that there is a constitution. The government is also limited, meaning that the government does not have complete control over the people. This means that the government must follow the law. Citizens themselves can vote when they are 18 or older.
