Economics

There are six main industries that are driving New Jersey's economy. These industries are life sciences, financial services, advanced manufacturing, information technology, and transportation and logistics. New Jersey's economy makes up around 3.2% of America's overall economy. Yet, New Jersey is ranked #31 for economy by usnews.com. Why is that? There are three main reasons for this: the business enviornment, employment rate, and economic growth. While New Jersey's buisness enviornment is relatively good, New Jersey's employment rate and economic growth are a bit lacking. New Jersey is ranked #22 for buisness enviornment and is ranked #36 for both employment rate and economic growth. Similar to the rest of America, New Jersey's government does not control the buisnesses.
