Religion

Though it's a very small state, especially compared to the rest of America, New Jersey has a very diverse span of cultures. With 54% of New Jersey's adults being religous it is the 19th most religous state in America. Most religous adults in New Jersey are Jewists, Hindus, Muslims, Buddhists, and Christians. Christians make make up most of the religious adults with 67% of them being believing in Christianity. New Jersey has a state religion. New Jersey's state religion is Christianity.
