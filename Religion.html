Religion

Though it's a very small state, especially compared to the rest of America, New Jersey hosts a variety of cultures. With 54% of New Jersey's adults being religous, it is the 19th most religous state in America. Most religous adults in New Jersey are either Jews, Hindus, Muslims, Buddhists, or Christians. About 67% of religous adults in New Jersey are Christian, making Christianity the most popular religion in the state. In fact, New Jersey is often referred to as a Christian island!
