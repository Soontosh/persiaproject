Area

Located in the northwestern hemisphere New Jersey has a normal climate. To be more specific New Jersey's average weather is between 44 degrees farenheit and 54 degrees farenheit. This is because it borders the Atlantic Ocean which moderates New Jersey's temperature. However, New Jersey's temperatures are rapidly increasing due to global warming. According to eia.gov just over 5% of New Jersey's electricity is made using renewable resources. This is not good compared to most other states in America. 60% of New Jersey is either low hills, pine forests, or salt marshes.
